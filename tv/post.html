MoboRemote

Many good ideas came to life for bad reasons, and this is one of them. Last winter, as my inner coach potatoes took over, I had a craving for a remote control app in order to change volume without having to move. Of course, I have no smart TV and watch videos on my PC and that is how MoboRemote idea was born. It is an mobile based app that allows users to remotely control their watching experience.
